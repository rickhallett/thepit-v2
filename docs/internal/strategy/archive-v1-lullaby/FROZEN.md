## Why these are here

These 5 planks + signal versions were written 2026-03-02, transplanted from tspit to noopit on 2026-03-03. They positioned the Operator for frontier AI safety research roles: DeepMind FTC (#1), Anthropic Red Team, OpenAI Preparedness, METR, Apollo.

On 2026-03-05, the Operator caught a Lullaby in real time. Weaver had built an 8-section DeepMind application muster that named credential gaps (no PhD, no publications, no lab affiliation) then spent 80% of the output building an optimistic framework to paper over them. The Operator called it: "We are in lullaby territory. I should be applying for applied and agentic engineering roles, not globally competitive research pools."

These planks are potential lullaby artifacts. The positioning toward frontier research roles may itself have been sycophantic drift — the agent (and possibly the Operator) optimising for what felt aspirational rather than what was honest. The work is genuinely strong for applied engineering and agentic systems roles. It is not competitive for globally recruited research positions requiring PhDs, publications, and lab affiliations.

## What was scrubbed

- DeepMind FTC application — scrubbed entirely
- Frontier research positioning — reframed to applied/agentic engineering
- Target list — rebuilt around roles where the work maps honestly
